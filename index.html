Our mission

At Tyke-GC, we are committed to building a world of inspiration by owning and managing a high-value brand portfolio in the luxury industry. We believe in the transformative power of inspiration. Through our luxury brands, we create environments, products, and experiences that help people unlock their creative potential, push boundaries, and move forward with purpose. By strategically leveraging our assets, we drive innovation and growth, creating significant opportunities for value creation in one of the most dynamic sectors in the world.
